Pokémon Information

Pokémon List

1. Pikachu

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon known for its yellow fur and ability to generate electricity.

2. Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon that evolves into Charmeleon and eventually into Charizard.

3. Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon with a plant bulb on its back that grows into a flower.

4. Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon known for its shell and ability to shoot water from its mouth.

5. Jigglypuff

Jigglypuff is a Normal/Fairy-type Pokémon famous for its ability to put others to sleep by singing.

6. Meowth

Meowth is a Normal-type Pokémon that is known for its love of shiny objects and its ability to speak human language in the anime.

7. Psyduck

Psyduck is a Water-type Pokémon known for its constant headache which gives it strong psychic abilities.
